Bolognese Sauce like Nona used to Make!

Ingredients:

  • 1lb Ground Chuck
  • 2 large white onions
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 stalk of celery
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • half a cup of white wine
  • half a cup of olive oil
  • 2 table spoon of chilli flakes
  • 2 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • 4 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • half a cup of pasta water
  • 1 anchovie
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions:

  1. Heat up large pot, cook ground beef on medium heat
  2. salt generously
  3. at about halfway cooked, add half of the chilli flakes, rosemary and thyme
  4. remove cooked beef, placing aside for later
  5. Heat up olive oil in the same pot
  6. finely chop onions and celery, shred carrots and add to heated pot
  7. fry on medium heal until browning, deglaze with white wine
  8. add chopped garlic, remaining chilli flakes, chopped rosemary and thyme
  9. cook until garlic is soft
  10. add cooked beef back in with the cooked veggies
  11. add tomato paste, cook for 2 minutes
  12. add tomato sauce and pasta water, and sugar
  13. let simmer for at least 30 minutes
  14. finish with butter, serve over your favourite pasta
