Perfect Buerre-Blanc everytime

Ingredients:

  • 1lb cold butter
  • 2 shallots
  • 4 ounces white wine
  • 6 ounces lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Finely chop shallots
  2. add shallots, white wine, and lemon juice to a cold pot
  3. heat up slowly on low heat, making sure not to boil
  4. cut cold butter into 16 small cubes
  5. once shallot/lemon/wine mixture is warm, add one cube of butter
  6. continuously stir until butter is incorporated
  7. continue adding in butter one cube at a time and emulsifying until no butter remains
  8. remove from heat, serve
