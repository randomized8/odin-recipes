Lemon Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 skinless boneless chiken breasts
  • 2 whole lemons
  • the zest of 1 whole lemon
  • 1 cup lemon juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • one tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a deep, oven-safe pan, add lemon zest, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, olive oil, chicken stock, and white wine
  2. stir until combined
  3. salt and pepper chicken generously
  4. add chicken to liquid mixture
  5. slice lemons, place on top of chicken
  6. place rosemary sprigs in pan with chicken
  7. bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 40 minutes or until the internal temperature is 160 degrees
  8. remove lemons and rosemary, serve
